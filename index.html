Blocker

Make Screen Time actually unbypassable.

Most people can defeat app blockers. Blocker makes it nearly impossible by holding your recovery password hostage until your blocking period ends.

How it works

  1. Create a dedicated Apple ID for Screen Time recovery
  2. Store the password here (you won't be able to see it)
  3. Schedule your blocking times
  4. When blocking starts, set Screen Time with a random PIN
  5. Password is only released when blocking ends
